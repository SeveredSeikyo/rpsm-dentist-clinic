About Us

Welcome to RPSM Dental Clinic, your trusted partner for comprehensive dental care in Tunasan, Muntinlupa City, Philippines. We are committed to providing a wide range of high-quality dental treatments to help you achieve and maintain a healthy, beautiful smile.

Our services include:

  • Scaling and Polishing
  • Braces Repair
  • Restoration
  • Artificial Teeth

We are open from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM and are located at 319 Arandia Street, Tunasan, Muntinlupa City. We also actively engage with our community through our Facebook page, where we share updates and information.

Please don't hesitate to contact us for appointments or any inquiries. We look forward to serving you!
